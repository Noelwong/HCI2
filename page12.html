Associate of Science
in Business Analysis

  • Programme Intended Learning Outcomes (PILOs)
  • Career Prospects
  • Main Subject Areas
  • General Education Courses
  • Mode of Study

Apply mathematical and statistical skills to formulate and solve business problems;
Use a range of statistical and information technology applications to solve business problems;
Apply business sense and ethical principles in the undertaking of statistical work;
Examine the major socio-political, cultural and economic issues affecting business in the local, national, regional and international contexts;
Perform skilled tasks responsibly and produce outputs that meet quality standards;
Communicate efficiently in English both orally and in writing in different business environments;
Relate the major ideas in arts and humanities, science and technology to their own area of study.

Graduates from this programme are eligible to apply for Data Analyst, Business Analyst, Market Analyst, Statistical Officer, Research Officer, Database Officer, and other positions that require data analysis in the banking, finance, insurance, business solutions and market research industries. Recent graduates are now employed in Standard Chartered, Hong Kong Telecom, SAS, Census & Statistics Department, etc.
